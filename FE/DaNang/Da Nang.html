    Da Nang

    Da Nang is one of five centrally-governed cities in Vietnam, located in the South Central Coast region. This is the central and largest city in the entire Central region, playing an important role as the nucleus of the Central Key Economic Zone. Da Nang City is currently a class 1 urban area, a central city at the regional and national levels.

    • Road system
    • Da Nang City connects with domestic localities through two national highways: National Highway 1 with the North-South Expressway in the East and National Highway 14B with the North-South Expressway in the West.
    • Airway system
    • Da Nang International Airport is one of the three largest and most important international airports in Vietnam, with 84 check-in counters and other modern facilities has ensured to serve more than 10 million passengers/year and 15 million passengers/year from 2020 onwards.
    • Waterway system
    • With a particularly favorable location for sea traffic, close to international shipping routes, Da Nang Port is the largest seaport in the Central region, capable of receiving large ships with a tonnage of up to 40,000 tons.
