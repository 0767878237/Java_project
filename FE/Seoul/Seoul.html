    Seoul

    Seoul, the capital of South Korea, is a vibrant city that seamlessly blends ancient traditions with cutting-edge modernity. Known for its historic landmarks like Gyeongbokgung Palace, Bukchon Hanok Village, and the iconic N Seoul Tower, the city is a treasure trove of cultural experiences.

    • Waterway system
