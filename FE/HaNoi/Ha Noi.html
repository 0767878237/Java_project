    Ha Noi

    Hanoi is the capital, a centrally governed city and one of two special urban areas of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam. This is the largest city in Vietnam, located as the political center, one of two important economic, cultural and educational centers in Vietnam. With terrain including the central plain and mountainous areas in the north and west of the city. With an area of 3,359.82 km², and a population of 8.4 million people, Hanoi is the largest centrally governed city in Vietnam, and is also the second most populous city and has the second highest population density among the 63 provincial administrative units of Vietnam.
