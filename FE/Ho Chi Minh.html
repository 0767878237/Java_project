    Ho Chi Minh

    Ho Chi Minh City is the largest city in Vietnam by population and serves as a center for economy and entertainment, as well as one of the two important cultural and educational centers in the country. It is a centrally governed city classified as a special urban area in Vietnam. This city currently has 16 districts, 1 city, and 5 rural areas, with a total area of 2,095 km².

    Tourist Attraction

    • Villa Sông
      Thao Dien, District 2
    • Villa Song Saigon is a white castle designed in French style located on the outskirts of Saigon River. With an extremely quiet location, Villa Song seems to be one of the resorts near Saigon that is perfect for an impromptu "getaway", full of comfort but not taking too much travel time.
    • Price: from 5.500.000 VND/night
    • Thao Dien Boutique Hotel
      197/1 Nguyen Van Huong, Thao Dien, District 2
    • Located just a few minutes drive from the city center, but with a completely quiet landscape, isolated houses, many lovely cafes, restaurants with a variety of European and Asian cuisines. Therefore, this is also a prime area for a resort paradise, concentrating high-class resorts near Saigon and ready for staycation trips.
    • Price: from 1.700.000 VND/night
    • Chloe Gallery
      06 Phan Van Chuong, Tan Phu, District 7
    • This place was once known as TajmaSago - a majestic white castle in the middle of Saigon. This place has always been a favorite resort destination for domestic and international tourists.
    • Price: From 2.380.560 VND/night
    • Airway system
    • Tan Son Nhat International Airport is the largest international airport in Vietnam. Tan Son Nhat International Airport with an area of 850 hectares is the largest airport in Vietnam in terms of area and terminal capacity.
    • This airport is the main operating place of all Vietnamese airlines, and is the headquarters of Vietnam Airports Corporation, the unit that manages all civil airports in Vietnam.
    • Ho Chi Minh City Urban Railway
    • The project is a combination of metro, tramway and monorail.
    • The system consists of 8 lines with a total length of 169 km, 1 tram line of 12.8 km and 2 monorail lines of 43.7 km long. There are 175 stations with a total system length of 225.5 km.
    • Waterway system
    • Waterway tourism becomes one of the types of tourism, tourism products that create a difference for the City, contributing to diversifying the City's tourism products. Strive for the total number of waterway transport vehicles serving tourists by 2025 to reach 200 canoes, 100 ships, boats and yachts of all kinds.
    • Landmark 81
      720A Dien Bien Phu, Ward 22
    • A super-tall skyscraper high (1,513 ft) in Ho Chi Minh City, part of the $1.4 billion Vinhomes Central Park project complex. Landmark 81 is the tallest building in Vietnam, the second tallest building in Southeast Asia, and the 17th tallest building in the world.
    • Bitexco Financial Tower
      2 Hai Trieu, Ben Nghe Ward, District 1
    • A skyscraper built in the center of District 1, the building is built on an area of nearly 6,100 m². The total investment capital is estimated at about 220 million USD, invested in construction by Bitexco Group headquartered in Hanoi.
    • Empire City Tower
      Empire City Urban Area, Thu Thiem, District 2
    • The building structure consists of 3 towers, apartment and office towers 34-88 floors high, the central tower 333m high is designed as a miniature natural garden blending in the middle of the clouds.
    • Ben Thanh Market
      Le Loi Street, Ben Thanh Ward, District 1
      Over hundreds of years of history, Ben Thanh Market is considered a special symbol of Saigon. The old architecture is still preserved until now, although Ben Thanh Market underwent a major reform and repair in 1985.
    • Independence Palace
      135 Nam Ky Khoi Nghia, Ben Thanh Ward, District 1
      The Independence Palace is a mansion in Ho Chi Minh City, which was the residence and workplace of the President of the Republic of Vietnam before the April 30, 1975 event. Currently, the palace has been ranked as a special national monument by the Vietnamese Government. The agency managing the Independence Palace cultural relic is called the Reunification Hall under the Administrative Department II - Government Office.
    • Immaculate Conception Cathedral Basilica
      1, Paris Commune, District 1
      Notre Dame Cathedral of Saigon is the cathedral of the Archdiocese of Saigon. It is one of the unique architectural works of Ho Chi Minh City and a famous destination for tourists. The total length of the cathedral is 93 m. The width at its widest point is 35 m. The height of the dome is 21 m. The capacity of the cathedral can reach 1,200 people.
