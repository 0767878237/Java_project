    Quang Ninh

    Quang Ninh is a coastal province in the Northeastern region of Vietnam. According to the economic development plan, Quang Ninh belongs to both the Northern Key Economic Zone and the Northern Coastal Region. This is the main coal mining province of Vietnam, with Ha Long Bay, a world heritage and natural wonder. Quang Ninh is also one of the eight provinces planned by the Vietnamese Government to become centrally-governed cities.
