    Da Nang

    Da Nang is one of five centrally-governed cities in Vietnam, located in the South Central Coast region. This is the central and largest city in the entire Central region, playing an important role as the nucleus of the Central Key Economic Zone. Da Nang City is currently a class 1 urban area, a central city at the regional and national levels.

    Tourist Attraction

    • InterContinental Danang Sun Peninsula Resort
      North Beach, Son Tra
    • Located on a hillside overlooking the Danang Bay - the area where this resort is located, InterContinental Danang Sun Peninsula Resort is perfect for travelers who want to enjoy a private space in a natural setting, away from the noise of the city.
    • Room rates from: 16.000.000 VND / night
    • Fusion Suites Danang Beach
      Vo Nguyen Giap, Man Thai ward, Son Tra
    • This is a quiet sea area with only fishermen pulling in their nets every morning. In addition to the infinity pool built on the sand, the rooftop area with open space and a view of the city is the most suitable place for couples looking for a romantic location in Da Nang.
    • Room rates from: 3.500.000 VND / night
    • Melia Danang Resort
      19 Truong Sa, Hoa Hai Ward, Hoa Hai, Ngu Hanh Son
    • Melia Danang Resort is not only one of the 10 luxury resorts in Danang but also in the top 10 hotels in Asia with many amenities. The total number of rooms is up to more than 150, helping this resort diversify rooms from mid-range to high-end, able to provide many services with the best quality.
    • Room rates from: 3.200.000 VND / night
    • Road system
    • Da Nang City connects with domestic localities through two national highways: National Highway 1 with the North-South Expressway in the East and National Highway 14B with the North-South Expressway in the West.
    • The inner-city traffic infrastructure is quite complete with a traffic network connecting to the city's ring roads, making Da Nang one of the few cities in Vietnam that rarely faces traffic jams.
    • Airway system
    • Da Nang International Airport is one of the three largest and most important international airports in Vietnam. Da Nang International Airport with 84 check-in counters and other modern facilities has ensured to serve more than 10 million passengers/year and 15 million passengers/year from 2020 onwards.
    • Waterway system
    • With a particularly favorable location for sea traffic, close to international shipping routes, Da Nang Port is the largest seaport in the Central region, capable of receiving large ships with a tonnage of up to 40,000 tons.
    • Da Nang Port continues to maintain its position as the number 1 port in the Central region and one of the largest and most modern seaports in Vietnam. Da Nang Port also contributes to the city's tourism development by welcoming 95 cruise ships with nearly 188,000 passengers and crew members.
    • Han River Bridge
      Bach Dang, Ward Hai Chau 1, Hai Chau District
    • Han River Bridge is one of the bridges across the Han River in Da Nang, Central Vietnam. This is the first swing bridge designed and constructed by Vietnamese engineers and workers and is the only swing bridge that can still rotate in Vietnam today.
    • Dragon Bridge
      Hai Chau District
    • Dragon Bridge is 666 m long and 37.5 m wide with 6 lanes, costing nearly 1.5 trillion VND (US$88m). The bridge was designed and built in the shape of a dragon that can breathe fire and water. Currently, the fire and water breathing time starts at 9:00 p.m. every Friday, Saturday, Sunday and major holidays.
    • Golden Bridge
      Empire City Urban Area, Thu Thiem, District 2
    • Golden Bridge is the name of a pedestrian bridge about 150 m (about 490 ft) long. Located at an altitude of about 1,414 m (above sea level) on Ba Na Mountain, the bridge connects the cable car station with other gardens of the resort. In the middle of the bridge are two large hands constructed by assembling the skeleton and plastering the outside to create a highlight for the bridge.
    • Sun World Ba Na Hills
      An Son Village, Hoa Ninh Commune, Hoa Vang District
    • Coming to Sun World Ba Na Hills, visitors will experience four seasons in one day, travel on cable cars, immerse themselves in the natural landscape of the primeval forests in Ba Na, enjoy diverse cuisine and enjoy the festive atmosphere.
    • Linh Ung Pagoda
      Bai But, Tho Quang ward, Son Tra district
    • Linh Ung Pagoda is one of three pagodas named Linh Ung in Da Nang. It is unclear whether it is by chance or fate that all three pagodas are located in prime locations in Da Nang city, forming a sacred "triangle" in the city.
    • Linh Ung Ba Na Pagoda is located on the top of the high mountain of the resort "Da Lat of Central Vietnam" and Linh Ung Bai But, Son Tra, is located halfway up the mountain - Son Tra peninsula. Linh Ung Bai But Pagoda is the largest, newest and most beautiful pagoda of the 3 pagodas.
    • Son Tra Peninsula
      54 Nguyen Luong Bang, Hoa Khanh Bac Ward, Lien Chieu District
    • The Also known as the “green fairy”, covering an area of 3,439 hectares, Son Tra Peninsula has a cool climate all year round and the most diverse flora and fauna ecosystem in Da Nang.
