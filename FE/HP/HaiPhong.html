    Hai Phong city

    Hai Phong is an important port city, industrial center, and the largest seaport in the North of Vietnam, as well as the economic, cultural, medical, educational, scientific, commercial and technological center of the country. Northern coastal region. This is the third largest city in the country, the second largest in the North after Hanoi

    • Hai Phong Airport or Catbi Airport (HPH), an international airport located 5 km from the city center. Cat Bi Airport was built by the French during the French colonial period
    • Hai Phong railWay
    • Hanoi - Hai Phong railway is a railway connecting the capital Hanoi with the port city of Hai Phong. This railway line is 102 km long, passing through 4 provinces and cities: Hanoi, Hung Yen, Hai Duong and Hai Phong.
